OUR BELIEFS


The General Overseer and the members of the executive council of the Christ City of Life Christian Centre Int’l, have taken in hand to set forth a declaring of twenty one doctrinal beliefs, most surely believed among us as necessary in understanding the intent of the living God.

DOCTRINAL BELIEFS


1. THE SCRIPTURES (THE HOLY BIBLE)
1Pt. 2:2, 2Pt. 1:21, 2Tim.3:15-17-
The Bible is our sole guide and sets forth criteria for a
heaven- bound people. It supremely declares the holy oracles of God.
The scripture, both Old and New Testament consisting of 66 books are inspired by the Holy Spirit.


2. THE GODHEAD
God is a Spirit who at divers times times
has manifested Himself to man as the Father, In Jesus Christ and the Holy Spirit.


3. THE DEITY OF THE LORD JESUS
Matt. 1:18,23 Lk.1:26-27 1Tim.2:5-
The Lord Jesus Christ is true God and true man,
haven been conceived by the Holy Spirit and born of the Virgin Mary.


4. THE ORIGINAL SIN AND FALL OF MAN
Rom.3:1-7 Gen.3:6-24 Rom.5:12-19-
That by the transgression of ome man Adam, sin entered into the world resulting in total
spiritual death, which is separation from God of the entire human race.


5. THE SUBSTITUTIONARY SACRIFICE OF CHRIST
Acts 2:32-36 1Pt. 2:24 Heb. 9:14 1Cor. 15:3-8 Heb.10:12-
Jesus died on the cross and shed His blood as a sacrifice for our sins. He arose bodily from the dead,
ascended to heaven and is seated at the right hand of the Majesty on high.


6. THE SALVATION OF MAN AND JUSTIFICATION BY GRACE THROUGH FAITH
Eph. 4:11-12 Rom. 3:3 Lk. 24:47-
Salvation, justification and regeneration is received through repentance and Godly sorrow for sin
and that as an act of God’s grace we receive forgiveness and remission of sin.


7. THE MINISTRY OF THE HOLY SPIRIT
Jon.15:26 Jon.16:8-14 Acts 1:8-
The ministry of the Holy Spirit to glorify Christ,
to convict men of sin, righteousness and judgment and to empower the believer. T
he Holy Spirit operates as the unlimited and invisible presence of Jesus Christ on the earth
as Comforter and Advocate indwelling
every born again believer and making the believer’s body His temple.


8. THE CHURCH
1Cor.12:12-17 Eph.1:22-23 Eph.2:22 Heb.12:23-
The true church is composed of such persons who,
through saving faith in Jesus Christ have been
regenerated by the Holy Spirit. The true church is the body of Christ,
not intended to be the edifice or the institution,
but the habitation of God through the Holy Spirit.
Each believer born of the Spirit is an integral part of the General Assembly
and the Church of the first born
which is written in heaven.


9. WATER BAPTISM
Matt.28:19 Mk.16:16-
The ordinance of baptism immersion in water is commanded by our Lord Jesus Christ.
That all who repent of their sins and believes in Christ as saviour and Lord are to be baptized.


10. THE PRIESTHOOD OF THE BELIEVERS
1Pt.2:5, Rom.12:1-
The believer is to offer unto God spiritual sacrifices because we have gained access
into God’s holy of holies through Christ.


11. THE HOLY COMMUNION OR THE LORD’S SUPPER
1Cor.11:26 Lk. 22:17-20 2Pt.1:4-
The Lord’s supper consisting of the elements,
bread and the cup of the fruit of the vines is the
expression of our sharing in the divine nature of our Lord Jesus Christ.
It is memorial of His sufferings and death.


12. THE BAPTISM OF THE HOLY SPIRIT
Acts 1:39 1Cor.14:2-18 Jude 20 1Cor.12:1-31-
The Spirit is sent to gift each believer with their own private prayer and praise language
used to edify themselves and that such baptism empowers the believer to
be an effective witness for Christ.


13. DIVINE HEALING AND MIRACLES
Exod.15:23-26 Mk.16:18 1Cor.12:8-10 Matt.16:17-
Divine healing was clearly provided for in the Old Testament and was an integral
part of the New Testament Gospel as taught and demonstrated by Jesus Christ,
the Apostles and the early church.


14. SANCTIFICATION
Heb.12:14 1Thess.5:23 2Pt.3:18 1Thess.4:3 1Pt.1:16-
Sanctification is definite yet progressive work of grace,
the separation from anything
considered sinful in the scriptures and of dedication to God.


15. THE FIVE-FOLD MINISTRY
Eph.4:11 1Cor.7:20 1Cor.12:28-
God by the Holy Spirit, definitely call
whom he desires to serve in the five-fold ministry and
qualifies the one called with power, talents, wisdom and gifts pertaining
to that office or offices.


16. MATRIMONY
Gen.2:24 Matt.19:5-6 –
Marriage between one man and one woman shall be binding for life.


17. TITHES AND OFFERINGS
Mal.3:8,10,11-
All Christians should tithe to the storehouse (Church),
where they receive spiritual food.


18. THE BLESSED HOPE-THE RESSURRECTION OF THE SAINTS
Acts1:11 1Thess.4:16,17 Rom.8:23 1Cor.15:51 Titus2:13-
The pre-millennial coming of our Lord Jesus Christ
in the clouds to catch away His Bride.


19. THE SECOND ADVENT OF CHRIST
1Thess.4:16 Zech.14:5 Matt.24:27,30 Rev.17 Rev.19:11,20 Matt.25:46
The second coming of Christ includes the catching away (Rapture) of the Church (Saints),
followed by the actual visible return of Christ with His Saints to
reign with Him on the earth for one thousand years.


20. ETERNAL HEAVEN AND ETERNAL HELL
Matt.25:46 Rev.19:11-15 Rev.21:8 Mk.9:43-48-
There will be a final judgment in which the wicked dead will be raised and judged according to their works.
Whosoever is not found written in the book of life,
together with the devil and his angels, the beast and the false
prophet will be consigned to everlasting punishment in
the lake which burns with fire and brimstone, which is the second death.


21. THE NEW HEAVEN AND THE NEW EARTH
2Pt.3:13 Rev.21:1-
We, according to His promise look for new heavens
and new earth wherein dwells righteousness.







CHRIST CITY OF LIFE...

We are defined not so much by WHO we are, our backgrounds or ethnicity, what we do, and where we live, but we are defined by WHOSE we are. That is, we are the church of Jesus Christ, because we belong to Him by grace through faith in Him. And as the church, we gather together and commit to one another for worship, for spiritual growth and encouragement, and for service and obedience to God.
